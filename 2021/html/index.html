Running

I often have thought about the question: what is it about running that makes me happy? I used to think that it was a basic chemical thing - nothing too profound except a boost in the happy feels juice. But the more I run, the more I think that's not the case. Sometimes I run and feel like shit, but I always look back on the experience as being good for my soul.
